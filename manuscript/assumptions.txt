# Assumptions

## Knows and Dont-Knows

* You **know** elementary HTML.
* You **know** elementary JavaScript.
* You **know** what the DOM is.
* You **don't** know anything about React.js.

## Light on Jargon

I won't use much jargon. Where there's verbiage you need to know, I'll ease into it.

If you want to half-learn terminology to impress your friends at a nerd cocktail parties, this book isn't for you. If you want to you want to full-learn terminology to shame your half-learned friends at a nerd cocktail party, this book is for you.

## HTML Starting Point

I want to get to React quickly. So, I'm starting with HTML. It'll be easy to apply what you learn to other platforms.
