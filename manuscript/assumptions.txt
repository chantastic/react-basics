# Assumptions & Goals

## What you know and don't know

* You **know** elementary HTML.
* You **know** elementary JavaScript.
* You **know** what the DOM is.
* You **don't** know anything about React.js.

## As little jargon as possible

I won't use much jargon. Where there's verbiage you need to know, I'll do my best to ease into it.

If you want to half-learn terms to impress your friends at a nerd cocktail party, this book isn't for you. If you want to you want to full-learn terms and shame your friends at a nerd cocktail party, this book is for you.

## Starting with HTML

I want to get to React as quickly. So, I'll start with HTML. It's easy and we can address other platforms in later.
